Biography

I am a scientist at Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL) working on applying ML/AI to particle accelerators. My research interests currently include physics-constrained machine learning, ML surrogate models and Bayesian optimization for acclerators.

Before joining LANL, I earned my Master's and Ph.D. degrees from Florida International Univeristy, where I worked in theoretical nuclear physics.
